EPC & Major Projects

Zero Liquid Discharge (ZLD)

We design and deliver world-class ZLD systems tailored for industries facing strict discharge norms. Our solutions combine cutting-edge Mechanical Vapor Recompression (MVR), Multi-Effect Evaporation (MEE), and advanced crystallization technologies to achieve 99%+ water recovery while minimizing operational cost.

  • • High water recovery rates, reducing freshwater dependency
  • • Energy-efficient MVR systems with AI-driven process optimization
  • • Salt recovery and crystallizer integration for zero discharge compliance
  • • Scalable modular design for easy capacity expansion
  • • Remote monitoring and predictive maintenance support
  • • Compliance with CPCB, SPCB, and international discharge norms

Whether for textile, chemical, pharma, or power sectors, our ZLD solutions ensure complete regulatory compliance, protect water resources, and contribute to your sustainability goals.

Sewage Treatment Plants (STP)

We design & commission STPs that consistently meet stringent discharge norms. Our solutions leverage advanced biological processes and membrane technologies for compact footprints, low operating cost, and reliable water reuse.

  • • MBR & MBBR technology for high-quality treated water
  • • Automated control systems for minimal operator intervention
  • • Odor control & sludge handling solutions for urban environments
  • • Energy-optimized designs reducing life cycle costs

What makes us different: Our STPs are future-ready with IoT-based remote monitoring, predictive maintenance, and provision for reuse in cooling towers, flushing, or irrigation.

Effluent Treatment Plants (ETP)

Every industry is unique — so are our ETP solutions. From physico-chemical treatment to advanced oxidation processes and membrane filtration, we deliver custom treatment trains that ensure full compliance and minimal downtime.

  • • COD, BOD, and TSS reduction with guaranteed performance
  • • Heavy metal recovery and recycling solutions
  • • Skid-mounted options for quick deployment
  • • Real-time online monitoring for SPCB/CPCB reporting

Our edge: We design for operational ease — lower chemical consumption, energy-efficient motors, and automated backwash to cut OPEX and keep plants running 24/7.

Water Treatment Plants (WTP)

From drinking water plants to ultrapure process water, we provide turnkey WTP solutions that deliver consistent quality under varying raw water conditions — helping industries maintain production without compromise.

  • • RO, UF/MF, and ion-exchange systems
  • • Automated dosing and online quality monitoring
  • • Containerized modular units for remote sites
  • • WHO & BIS compliance for potable water

Why choose us: Our WTPs are designed for minimal water rejection, low energy consumption, and easy operation — lowering total cost of ownership.

Common Effluent Treatment (CETP)

We specialize in designing and operating CETPs that serve entire industrial clusters. Our focus is on cost sharing, centralized monitoring, and regulatory compliance — helping industries meet norms collectively, affordably, and sustainably.

  • • Optimized hydraulic & biological load balancing
  • • Economies of scale to reduce per-unit treatment cost
  • • Real-time SCADA-based monitoring and reporting
  • • Resource recovery and sludge valorization

Our differentiator: Proven track record in managing CETPs for 50+ industries, ensuring zero non-compliance penalties and sustainable operation.
