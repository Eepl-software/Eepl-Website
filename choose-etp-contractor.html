How to Choose an ETP Plant Contractor

A strong ETP plant contractor should understand wastewater chemistry, regulatory compliance, civil and mechanical execution, automation, commissioning and long-term O&M. For industrial projects, EPC accountability matters as much as equipment supply.

Selection criteria

Check relevant industry experience, treatability evaluation, performance commitments, commissioning support and AMC capability before finalizing a contractor.
